I'm Francis Kwadzo Amuzu, a Hybrid Accounting & Technology professional based in Accra, Ghana. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Administration (Accounting Option) from the University of Ghana, Legon, and I'm passionate about combining financial expertise with technical capabilities to drive operational efficiency.

My experience spans estate accounts administration at the Registrar General's Department, banking operations at GCB Bank PLC, and financial management as Financial Secretary at LifeCity Church. What sets me apart is my ability to spearhead cloud-based data management systems, develop web-based accounting solutions, and automate financial records — all while maintaining high standards of regulatory compliance and financial accountability.
